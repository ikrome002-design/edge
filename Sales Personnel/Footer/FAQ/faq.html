Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Edge by Citrus FAQ. Below you will find answers to common questions to help you make the most of our SaaS webapp.

1. General Information

Q: What is Edge by Citrus?

A: Edge by Citrus is a state-of-the-art SaaS webapp developed by Citrus Labs designed to streamline business operations and enhance productivity through secure, intuitive, and innovative digital solutions.

2. Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account on Edge by Citrus?

A: Accounts are created directly by Citrus Labs. You will receive your account details via email from Citrus Labs.

Q: How do I log in to my account?

A: To log in, use your work email address. A verification URL will be sent to your email—simply click the link to access your account.

3. Features and Functionality

Q: What are the key features of Edge by Citrus?

A: Our webapp offers an intuitive dashboard, robust data analytics, real-time notifications, secure user management, and various tools designed to optimize your workflow. For a detailed list, please refer to our Features section within the app.

Q: How do I access and use these features?

A: Once logged in, navigate through the main menu to explore the available features. Each section includes detailed instructions and help resources for ease of use.

4. Account Management

Q: How can I update my account information?

A: Log in and visit the Account Settings page to update your personal details, contact information, and preferences.

Q: What should I do if I forget my password?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered work email to reset your password.

Q: Who do I contact for account-related issues?

A: For any account issues, please reach out to our customer support using the contact details provided in the Customer Support section below.

5. Data Privacy and Security

Q: How does Edge by Citrus protect my personal data?

A: We adhere strictly to the Data Protection Act, No. 24 of 2019. Your data is secured using state-of-the-art encryption, robust access controls, and regular security audits to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Q: Where can I find detailed information on data privacy?

A: Detailed data privacy and security information is available at the footer of the Edge by Citrus website.

6. Troubleshooting

Q: What should I do if I experience technical issues?

A: First, try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If issues persist, please consult our troubleshooting guides or contact customer support for assistance.

Q: How can I report a bug or technical problem?

A: Use the User Feedback section within the webapp to report any issues. Please include as much detail as possible to help us resolve the problem quickly.

8. Legal and Compliance

Q: Where can I find the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy?

A: The Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and other legal documents are located at the footer of the Edge by Citrus website.

9. User Feedback

Q: How can I provide feedback or suggest improvements?

A: We value your feedback. Please use the feedback form available within the webapp or contact our customer support to share your suggestions.

Q: What should I do if I want to report a bug?

A: Report bugs via the User Feedback section in the app. Detailed reports help us improve the service and resolve issues promptly.
